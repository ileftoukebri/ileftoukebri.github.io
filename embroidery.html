THREADPLAY

Threadplay is a series of handmade modern embroideries that break away from the ordinary. Each piece begins with a single thread, slowly growing into something expressive, textured, and richer than traditional embroidery.

THREADPLAY TECHNIQUES

From abstract forms to layered textures, this series explores mixed-thread embroidery using DMC threads, wool, beads, knots, and experimental stitching.

IMAGINATION & NATURE

Shapes and textures are inspired by nature and free imagination. Each piece combines threads, knots, beads, and creative stitching to form playful, abstract compositions.
