SWAY

Sway is a series of porcelain cups born from hands-on craftsmanship and playful imagination.
Each piece is shaped by hand to fit naturally in your fingers.

Each cup is carefully poured into plaster molds, allowing the porcelain to take on soft, organic shapes.
The handmade process embraces imperfections and subtle textures, giving every cup a unique character and tactile beauty.

Once removed from the mold, the cups are refined and smoothed by hand, then finished with a Chinese-inspired red-purple glaze. The final forms are playful and ergonomic, designed to fit naturally in the hand while showcasing the texture, color, and craftsmanship that make each cup a functional piece of art
