About Me

From Tunisia to Budapest, and from numbers to design, I followed my heart. An art-oriented soul with roots that nurture my visual language to transform stories, memories, and emotions into tangibility.

Trained in product design, but an artist at heart, my work bridges the worlds of functional design and creative expression. It moves across disciplines: product design, multidisciplinary art, and visual storytelling, always guided by curiosity, color, and a sense of creativity.
